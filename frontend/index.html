Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

25 mins
2 servings
4.8 (124 reviews)

A nutritious and colorful bowl packed with protein-rich quinoa, fresh vegetables, creamy feta cheese, and a zesty lemon dressing. Perfect for meal prep!

Vegetarian Gluten-Free Heart Healthy

Ingredients

1 cup quinoa
In pantry
2 cups cherry tomatoes
In pantry
1 cucumber, diced
In pantry
100g feta cheese
Need to buy
1/4 cup olive oil
In pantry
2 lemons, juiced

Instructions

1

Cook the quinoa

Rinse quinoa and cook according to package instructions. Let cool.

2

Prepare vegetables

Dice cucumber, halve cherry tomatoes, and crumble feta cheese.

3

Make dressing

Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

4

Combine and serve

Mix quinoa with vegetables, top with feta, and drizzle with dressing.
